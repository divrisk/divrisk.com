Administrative and Support Services

Any routine support services provided to businesses, from waste disposal to staffing, are categorized into the administrative and support and waste management and remediation services industry. A branch of the professional and business services super sector, this industry encompasses a vast array of business types that all serve as the backbone for both small and large enterprises.

 

The industry includes categories such as:

  • Administrative and support services
  • Waste management and remediation services

Facts about the administrative and support and waste management and remediation services industry:

  • In the beginning of 2017, the industry employed 9,171,300 individuals (Bureau of Labor Statistics)
  • The most common occupations in the industry are grounds maintenance workers, janitors and building cleaners, and security guards and gaming surveillance officers (Census Bureau)
  • In 2016, there were approximately 527,152 private establishments in the industry (Bureau of Labor Statistics)

 

The administrative and support and waste management and remediation services industry provides support for the daily operations of businesses. Since daily operations can pertain to such a wide spectrum, there are various coverage options available for every type of business in the industry.

For those involved in waste management, pollution liability is important in the event that liability suits arise from an error made during waste disposal. Enterprises providing services that require access to customer information and operations benefit from products such as fidelity bond insurance since they cover lawsuits arising from an employee engaging in a criminal act.
